ü™ü Windows 11: Microsoft Family Safety

English Instructions

  1. Ensure your Microsoft account is an administrator account.
  2. Go to Settings > Accounts > Family & other users.
  3. Click Add account and select Create one for a child.
  4. Enter your child's email, password, name, and birthday.
  5. After setup, go to Manage family settings online or remove an account.
  6. From the online dashboard, you can:
  1. Set screen time limits.
  2. Block inappropriate websites and apps.
  3. Approve app requests.
  4. Set spending limits in the Microsoft Store. (How to Set up Parental Controls in Windows 11)

üçé macOS: Screen Time & Family Sharing

English Instructions

  1. Open System Settings and click your Apple ID.
  2. Select Family > Set Up Family and follow the prompts to add your child.
  3. Once added, go to System Settings > Screen Time.
  4. Choose your child's name to configure:
  1. Downtime schedules.
  2. App limits.
  3. Communication restrictions.
  4. Content & Privacy Restrictions. (How to Use Parental Controls in Your Google, Apple, and Microsoft Accounts, How to Block Websites on the iPhone, Google Family Link: What It Is and How to Use It)

ü§ñ Android: Digital Wellbeing & Parental Controls

English Instructions

  1. Open Settings > Digital Wellbeing & parental controls.
  2. Tap Parental controls > Get started.
  3. Choose Child or teen, then Next.
  4. Sign in with your child's Google account or create one.
  5. Sign in with your parent Google account to link them.
  6. You can now:
  1. Set screen time limits.
  2. Approve or block apps.
  3. Manage web filters.
  4. Track device location. (Expert Tips to Monitor and Limit Screen Time on Android Phones, Google Family Link: What It Is and How to Use It, How to Set up Parental Controls in Windows 11, Google Family Link, Microsoft family features)

üì± iPhone: Screen Time

English Instructions

  1. Go to Settings > Screen Time.
  2. Tap Turn On Screen Time, then Continue.
  3. Select This is My Child's iPhone.
  4. Set a Screen Time passcode.
  5. Configure:
    1. Downtime.
    2. App Limits.
    3. Communication Limits.
    4. Content & Privacy Restrictions.
  6. To block websites:
  1. Go to Content & Privacy Restrictions > Content Restrictions > Web Content.
  2. Choose Limit Adult Websites or Allowed Websites Only. (Expert Tips to Monitor and Limit Screen Time on Android Phones, How to Use Parental Controls in Your Google, Apple, and Microsoft Accounts, How to Block Websites on the iPhone)
